Kelferria is a fire genasi woman with copper-bronze skin and a broad, smith's build, her hair a living corona of ember-orange flame tied back for the forge. She wears a fireproof variant of [[House Mabon | Mabon]] work dress: deep indigo wool and enchanted leather, a split apron, reinforced gauntlets tucked at her belt, and steel-blue maker's bindings at the wrists — practical layers that leave her hands free. She is as overworked but unbowed, a master craftswoman with no patience for small talk while an order backlog looms. She speaks bluntly and professionally, measuring clients the way she measures plate.

[[Factor | Factor]] of [[House Mabon | House Mabon]] (rank: Craftsman); keeps a handful of apprentices.

You see a compact black cat with white socks and amber eyes — soft-footed, watchful, fur well-kept for a street animal. They present as calm and diplomatic among their own kind, the cat who was sent for when politeness failed. Through Ros's magic they communicate in careful, deliberate images: old fear, new predator, a request for aid.

<!-- OP_BIO -->

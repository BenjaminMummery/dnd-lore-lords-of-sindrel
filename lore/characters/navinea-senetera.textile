Navinea is an enormous earth genasi woman whose skin is the colour of weathered clay — brown-grey, faintly granular — with a broad-shouldered, heavyset build. Her hair is coarse and iron-grey, cropped short like bundled mineral fibre. She wears [[House Grimbolg | Grimbolg]] work dress even at council: moss-green wool and bark-brown linen in layered, reinforced panels, a leather apron stamped with green-black House knotwork, heavy boots, and work gloves — the cut of a Factor who still looks ready to walk into a waterworks failure. She presents as unhurried and contemplative, weighing every answer as though the city were a machine that might fail if given incorrect information. She speaks slowly and deliberately, blunt when she reaches a conclusion.

<!-- OP_BIO -->
[[Factor | Factor]] of [[House Grimbolg | House Grimbolg]]. [[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Ros]]'s insight into interconnected natural systems is of particular interest; Navinea intends to organise a roundtable with Ros and senior transmuters.

She is concerned with system failure, entropy, and [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]'s resource vulnerability.

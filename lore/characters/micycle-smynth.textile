At the [[Osteomantic Archives | Wallarch Osteomantic Archives]], you see the mortal remains of an elderly human man of medium build. Heavily lined olive skin beneath close-cropped dark hair turned white at the temples. He lies in a plain linen shroud.

<!-- OP_BIO -->
* *29th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*[1]
** [[The Adventurers | The Wonch]] witnessed his funeral at the [[Osteomantic Archives | Wallarch Osteomantic Archives]] (early afternoon).
** Archive staff including [[:seelie-ophelan | Seelie Ophelan]] and [[:temperance-halke | Temperance Halke]] collected his remains after the rite; the party's departure from the chamber lacked dignity.

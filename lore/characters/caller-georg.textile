Johann Georg is a [[Factor | Factor]] of [[House Beltus | House Beltus]].

<!-- OP_BIO -->
h3. Death at the Beltan Institute

Caller Johann Georg's final summoning took place at the [[Beltan Institute | Beltan Institute]], [[House Beltus | House Beltus]]'s official centre for regulated summoning and pact-work. The demon's escape was therefore not a private magical mishap, but a disaster inside one of Beltus's most prestigious institutions.

After possessing Georg, the demon drove him through the Institute's portrait-lined halls, defacing portraits and precious items before Georg's heart finally gave out.

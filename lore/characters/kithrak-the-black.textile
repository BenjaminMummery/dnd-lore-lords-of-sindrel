Kithrak the Black, scourge of Arkay, the starless shadow, ruinous lord of the North.

<!-- OP_BIO -->
Ancient black dragon whose territory spanned much of the [[Arkay Mountain Range | Arkay Mountain range]]; ultimately defeated by the mages who would become the founders of [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]. His bones are displayed in the [[Osteomantic Archives | Osteomantic Archives]].

* *29th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*
** [[The Adventurers | The Wonch]] passed through the Hall of Aberrations during their catacomb expedition and recognised the colossal skeleton (late afternoon).[1]

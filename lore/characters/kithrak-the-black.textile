Kithrak the Black, scourge of Arkay, the starless shadow, ruinous lord of the North.

<!-- OP_BIO -->
Kithrak the Black was a colossal and ancient Black Dragon whose territory spanned much of the [[Arkay Mountain Range | Arkay Mountain range]]. He was ultimately defeated by the mages who would go on to become the founders of Sindrel. His bones are displayed in the [[Osteomantic Archives | Osteomantic Archives]].

Black-robed attendant at the [[Osteomantic Archives | Wallarch Osteomantic Archives]].

<!-- OP_BIO -->
*29th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*

Present at the funeral of [[:micycle-smynth | Micycle Smynth]], presided over by the [[:celebrant-alruna | celebrant]]. [[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Ros]] noticed two figures in archive robes among the mourners; at the rite's end, Seelie and [[:temperance-halke | Temperance Halke]] gathered the mortal remains and carried them toward the undercroft.

<!-- GM_INFO_START -->
Full name: Seelie Ophelan

Suggested house affiliation (index when revealed): [[House Goela | House Goela]]. Junior curator attached to [[:celebrant-alruna | Alruna]] — rites, vestments, and the liturgical side of interment. The archives are house-independent on paper; Ophelan was seconded from Goela's bridge archive as part of a long-standing exchange. Her given name is family tradition, not fey heritage.

Works alongside [[:temperance-halke | Temperance Halke]], who handles physical cataloguing and bone-work.

You see a human woman with dark skin and a slender, upright build, her head shaved smooth. She wears [[House Beltus | Beltus]] formal dress: a long black silk robe with gold embroidery at collar and cuffs, sheer smoke-coloured veils at the shoulders, and a high starched collar — the sort of garment that implies unseen servants helping her through every doorway. A mephit perches on her shoulder, the mark of a Beltus summoner. She presents as effortlessly confident and personable, though irritation shows quickly when bureaucracy wastes her time. Her voice stays soft and even, yet carries an authority that expects to be heard without repetition.

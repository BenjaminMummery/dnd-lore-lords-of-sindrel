Caller Alandria dresses formally in a pristine robe with a starched collar – black with golden trim. If the colours left any doubt as to her House Affiliation, the Mephit perched on her shoulder dispels it - only the summoners of [[House Beltus | House Beltus]] employ such servants.

She carries herself with the easy confidence of someone accustomed to power. Her voice is soft, but authoritative - she expects to be listened to without having to raise it. Despite her authority she is personable, even charming.

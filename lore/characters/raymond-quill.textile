Large, burly, and a complete bastard

<!-- OP_BIO -->
*  *4218 [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]]*: Along with his brother [[:gillette-quill | Gillette]], Raymond spends most of the year carrying out a series of petty and uninspired thieveries targetting travellers up and down [[The Mage Road | The Mage Road]].
* *24th of Enean 4218 [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]]*: The brothers are interrupted by [[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin Ravenglade]] while plotting to rob the patrons of [[The White Dragon Coaching Inn | The White Dragon Coaching Inn]]. During the altercation, Raymond is incapacitated by [[The Adventurers | the Adventurers]]. That evening he is guarded by [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Nakoma Deathwalker Mor'got'ha]] and [[:silrie-aegiskiir | Silrie Aegiskiir]], the latter of whom attempts to teach him the basics of meditation.
* *25th of Enean 4218 [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]]*: Now under the custody of the Adventurers, Raymond rides the coach to [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] while trying to convey his new meditation knowledge to Gillette. During the Anathemist ambush, he remains pinned down in the coach unwilling to risk the archers above.

h2. Target of the [[Ditchquer | Ditchquer]] Heist

Silas's rooms were the true target of the [[Anathemists | Anathemist]] operation during the [[Ditchquer | Ditchquer]] closing ceremony.

[[:barnabus | Barnabus]] pickpocketed Silas's key at the party and passed it to Arouraios, who was disguised as a coachman. [[:georges-de-selve | Georges de Selve]] and [[:jean-de-dinteville | Jean de Dinteville]] collected the key, then used it with [[:nielsenn | Nielsenn's]] locksmith skills to access Silas's rooms through the [[Osteomantic Archives | archive]] route while [[House Oestra | House Oestra]]'s attention was drawn away by [[The Fulcrum | the Fulcrum]] explosives.

The conspirators copied what they could and stole what they could not. The exact stolen material remains a key unresolved point, but the breach is directly connected to the [[Anathemists | Anathemists]]' knowledge of [[Kosmios | Kosmios]].

h2. Silas's awareness (Session 12)

* **Ditchquer night:** [[:barnabus | Barnabus]] lifted his key. Silas noticed the loss later; had it reissued. Did not search his rooms thoroughly - nothing looked disturbed.
* **After the captured Anathemist interrogation:** general intelligence suggested a high-level security breach somewhere in the Houses.
* **Silas's audit:** found **missing files** from his chambers. He now knows the breach was **his** - his key, his custody, his failure to report.
* **Current state:** furious that [[The Adventurers | the Wonch]] have trampled Oestra security (catacomb exit); **terrified** that anyone else will learn he lost classified material. Will not volunteer key loss, file theft, or Kosmios.

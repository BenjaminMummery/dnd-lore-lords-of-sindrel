You see a lean domestic cat with soot-grey tabby fur and pale green eyes — rangy muscle under a dusty coat, one ear nicked, no collar. They move with the unhurried confidence of a predator that owns the alley. When magic bridges the communication gap, they present as grave and urgent, a scout delivering bad news. They "speak" in terse impressions and sharp images rather than polished sentences.

<!-- OP_BIO -->
A [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] street cat. Part of the informal network known to [[:amanira | Amanira]] as the City Cats.

A half-orc sporting an eyepatch and a neatly trimmed goatee tinged with white.

Even within the city they wear half plate armour and carry a longsword.

They speak rarely and abruptly, preferring to let their sword do the communication. They constantly give the impression of having been dragged away from much more important and rewarding affairs.

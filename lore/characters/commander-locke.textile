The Oestran representative is a half-orc with grey-green skin and a heavy, muscular build; their scalp is shaved close, their goatee neatly trimmed and streaked with white, and an eyepatch covers one eye. They wear [[House Oestra | Oestra]] armour even indoors: dark steel half plate with the House mark across the breast, black leather and bronze trim, a crimson cloak clasped at one shoulder to leave the sword arm free, and a longsword worn openly at the hip. They present as permanently interrupted — terse, martial, visibly eager to be elsewhere. When they speak at all, it is in abrupt, economical phrases, as though conversation were a duty poorly discharged.

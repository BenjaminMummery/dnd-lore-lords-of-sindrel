You see an elf woman with pale gold skin and a tall, straight-backed build, her silver-white hair braided for ceremony and her face bare. She wears black robes layered over white linen - the colours of the Archive workers, although the cut and hang of the fabric are noticably [[House Goela | Goelan]]. A silver clasp at the shoulder, and a sheer dark veil pushed back from her brow complete the look — formal, sombre, precise.

She speaks in measured liturgical cadence, each phrase placed like a stone - the officiant of a rite that must not be interrupted.

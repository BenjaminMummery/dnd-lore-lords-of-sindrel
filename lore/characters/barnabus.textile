h3. Role in the Anathemist Plot

Barnabus is part of the Anathemist spy cell embedded in [[:regnif-d-log | Regnif d'Log]]'s household, outwardly serving as d'Log's butler. He is one of the cell's most important access agents because his role lets him move through respectable domestic, commercial, and servant spaces without seeming out of place.

Barnabus acts as the cell's fixer, handler, and domestic infiltrator. He exploits the invisible authority of service: appointments, letters, errands, household accounts, livery contacts, deliveries, tradespeople, and servants' gossip. He does not need to force access when he can arrange it as part of somebody else's routine.

Supposedly acting as [[:regnif-d-log | Regnif d'Log]]'s agent, Barnabus made a deal with [[:kelferria-the-smith | Kelferria]] of [[House Mabon | House Mabon]] for a large order of enchanted armour. He maked a large down payment and promised much more, but the samples were the true objective. The armour served two purposes: protection within the [[Quarantine Zone | Quarantine Zone]], allowing the cell to use The [[Quarantine Zone | Sacrum]] as a staging ground, and disguise. Because Kelferria supplies [[House Oestra | House Oestra]], the chest-plates can pass as Oestra armour at a distance once crudely decorated with the Oestra crest.

In the sabotage campaign before the [[Ditchquer | Ditchquer]] closing ceremony, Barnabus used servant networks to learn which households, [[Factor | Factors]], and House officials are vulnerable to distraction or theft. He helped identify [[:nielsenn | Nielsenn]] as a useful locksmith and helped arrange the circumstances that allow him to be kidnapped and his death staged. He also hid suspicious purchases inside d'Log's household accounts, including unusually high charcoal use at the lodgings.

During the [[Ditchquer | Ditchquer]] closing ceremony, Barnabus stole [[:silas-oestra | Silas Oestra]]'s key and passes it to  [[:arouraios | Arouraios]], who was disguised as a coachman. A brief exchange between two servants passed beneath the notice of anyone in the room. This theft enabled [[:georges-de-selve | Georges de Selve]], [[:jean-de-dinteville | Jean de Dinteville]], and [[:nielsenn | Nielsenn]] to access [[:silas-oestra | Silas Oestra]]'s rooms.

After the [[Ditchquer | Ditchquer]] operation, Barnabus remained in [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] as part of d'Log's retinue, permitted to stay until his master's business is concluded. This makes him the spy cell's remaining embedded agent after Georges and Jean depart.

h4. Investigative footprint

Evidence pointing to Barnabus may include household account irregularities, messages sent in d'Log's name, servant testimony, the Kelferria armour order, charcoal purchases, unexplained knowledge of [[:silas-oestra | Silas Oestra]]'s movements, and any clue showing that d'Log's authority was used without d'Log's understanding.

<!-- OP_DESCRIPTION -->
Pthul is a large, heavily built man. Stern, serious, and stoic to the point of being almost monosyllabic, he is often perceived as standoffish. His habitual dark clothing and ever present sword do little to assuage this appearance. In reality he is simply mentally categorising everything around him by threat level.

Pthul is utterly devoted to his charge, [[:dulcinia-von-phthasia | Dulcinia von Phthasia]].

<!-- OP_BIO -->
* *10th Enean 4218 [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]]*: Pthul and [[:dulcinia-von-phthasia | Dulcinia]] depart the von Phthasia estate in [[Soulwind | Soulwind]] with the goal of travelling to [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] to seek a magical cure for her ailment. The going is slow due to Dulcinia's ill health and the large amount of gold and jewels they are transporting.
* *24th Enean 4218 SC*: Alerted to the sinister motives of the [[:gillette-quill | Quill]] [[:raymond-quill | Brothers]], Pthul fights alongside a ragtag group of adventurers to subdue the ne'er-do-wells. At his mistresses insistence, he grudgingly allows his new allies to share their table for the evening.
* *25th Enean 4218 SC*: Pthul and Dulcinia's coach is ambushed by [[Anathemists | Anathemists]].

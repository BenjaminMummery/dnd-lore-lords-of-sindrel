Nans is a lean elf woman with medium-brown skin and a neat, economical build, her black hair drawn into a tight bun. She wears [[The Fulcrum | Fulcrum]] service livery: a pale marble-grey tunic and apron over practical black trousers, soft-soled shoes, and the eight-pointed star embroidered at the collar. She presents as watchful and professionally discreet — the sort of servant who notices everything and comments on little. She speaks carefully, in short, exact sentences, as though every word might be quoted later.

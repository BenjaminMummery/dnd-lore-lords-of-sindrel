Small, wily, and a complete bastard

<!-- OP_BIO -->
*  *4218 [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]]*: Along with his brother [[:raymond-quill | Raymond]], Gillette spends most of the year carrying out a series of petty and uninspired thieveries targetting travellers up and down [[The Mage Road | The Mage Road]].
* *24th of Enean 4218 [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]]*: The brothers are interrupted by [[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin Ravenglade]] while plotting to rob the patrons of [[The White Dragon Coaching Inn | The White Dragon Coaching Inn]]. During the altercation, Gillette is knocked unconscious.
* *25th of Enean 4218 [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]]*: Now under the custody of [[The Adventurers | the Adventurers]], Gillette rides the coach to [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]. During the Anathemist ambush, he remains pinned down in the coach unwilling to risk the archers above.

A gruff half-elf with short-cropped hair. His knuckles bear the scars of frequent sword training.

Hielaman carries a kite shield and short-sword. He speaks gruffly and authoritatively - a man accustomed to command.

<!-- OP_BIO -->
Hielaman is a [[Factor | Factor]] of [[House Meness | House Meness]]. He fulfills a role similar to 'captain of the guard'.

*26th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*

- An explosion is observed on the plane outside [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]. Hielaman and his squad are dispatched via portal to investigate.
- On arrival, they encounter the aftermath of [[The Adventurers | The Neighbourhood Wonch]]'s conflict with a small force of [[Anathemists | Anathemists]].
- Hielaman takes custody of the surviving [[:arise-to-victory-over-the-infidel-hoards | Anathemist Leutenant]], receives the [[:private-warwick-s-report | report]] of [[:sebastian-warwick | Private Sebastian Warwick]], and escorts the Wonch back to Sindrel.

*28th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*

- Encountering [[:pin | Pin]] in a bar, Hielaman enters into a heated discussion of the performance of his favoured Diechquer team - The Griffon's Talons. This discussion devolves rapidly, and Hieleman and Pin part on acrimonious terms, the former receiving a split lip to match the latter's black eye.

*29th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*

- Hielaman's interrogation of [[:arise-to-victory-over-the-infidel-hoards | the Anathemist Leutenant]] having revealed that the Anathemists have access to privileged information, a [[Hexine Circle | Hexine Circle]] is convened. Unable to reach resolution, they enlist [[The Adventurers | The Wonch]] to root out the spies they suspect have infiltrated the city.

That requires a lot of familiarity with the city. Why start from scratch when you can use whats already here? They’re the weak link, find them and lean on them.

Should lead to the quills, or Nakoma’s new friends. Either have contacts who know that _somebody_ was muscling in on the garbage collection - usually the domain of crime syndicates since it's a good way to move things without being noticed. Enough prodding will lead them to a new garbage service, actually a few local ruffians who found a cart abandoned just inside outside the quarantine zone.

This leads them to the quarantine zone, but they'll need magical defences to get far without dying.

If they get it, they can find the spies headquarters, now abandoned. Much of the evidence has been burned but there's a scrap of paper with House Oestra's mark.

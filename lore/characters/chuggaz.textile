You see a stocky dragonborn man with bronze scales and a broad-shouldered, work-hardened build, his horned head and dorsal frill catching the light. He wears [[The Fulcrum | Fulcrum]] service livery: the same pale marble-grey tunic and apron as the rest of the tower staff, black trousers, soft shoes, and the eight-pointed star at the collar. He presents as watchful and streetwise — friendly enough on the surface, always measuring who is asking and why. He answers in a low, careful voice, giving no more detail than the question seems to deserve.

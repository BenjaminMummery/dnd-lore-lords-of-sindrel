You see a kenku with a compact, quick-fingered build, their plumage ink-blue from crown to flight feathers and their beak dark and slightly scuffed from workshop work. Enchanted tinkerer's goggles — brass-rimmed lenses that faintly glow when they focus — sit pushed up on their brow or dropped over their eyes. They wear [[House Mabon | Mabon]] craft dress: layered indigo wool and storm-grey linen in an asymmetrical cut, a reinforced work apron, and a broad sash — sleeves rolled, hands left free, maker's marks on the clasps. They present as eager to help and visibly short on social confidence, lighting up when a problem turns technical. They speak in enthusiastic bursts stitched from borrowed workshop phrases, veering into cryptic jargon about "force multipliers" when weapons or mechanisms are mentioned.

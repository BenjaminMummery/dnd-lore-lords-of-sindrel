bq). Incidents, the last months. Misfortune maybe, sabotage we fear. Appearing random, but always a design beneath. The random hides the pattern. Find the parts that do not fit, pull on them until deception unravels.

Suspected or confirmed sabotage:
* A Tyromancer poisoned by his own cheese - overdose of halucinagens. (Goela)
* Magical locksmith vaporised when one of his own traps misfired. ([[House Mabon | House Mabon]])
* A series of explosive devices were found in the sewers below [[The Fulcrum | The Fulcrum]] on the night of the [[Ditchquer | Ditchquer]] tournament closing ceremony. One detonated early, causing some minor structural damage. The others were discovered and diffused. High ranking Factors of each house were in attendance. Reinforcements from [[House Oestra | House Oestra]] were called in to support the [[House Meness | House Meness]] guards already in place.
* A hydromancer drowned when the magical control rod for one of [[House Grimbolg | House Grimbolg]]'s water generators was inverted. The water container to overflowed and ruined several storage silos. They're still clearing it up. (this hid the theft of a large quantity of saltpetre (Potassium nitrate)).
* A summoner of [[House Beltus | House Beltus]] got possessed by his own demon, which ran amock through the halls of their house before being banished. The page of his grimoire showing the appropriate summoning circle for a succubus was found to have been vandalised. As a final sick joke, the demon enchanted its own sulphurous leavings which, when gathered up with the rest of the day's refuse, exploded (this last was _not_ the demon, but covered the theft of the sulfur).
* [[House Lithra | House Lithra]] The punch ladle at a diplomatic function was replaced with one enchanted with a geis of truth speaking. This resulted in three major diplomatic incidents, the end of two marriages, and a very awkward proposal.
* [[House Meness | House Meness]] - A high level enchanter was commissioned anonymously to place a spell of reflection on an amulet. The amulet had _already_ been enchanted with a spell of reflection, causing the spell to rebound. In a city where magic is everywhere, he's a walking danger to everyone around him.

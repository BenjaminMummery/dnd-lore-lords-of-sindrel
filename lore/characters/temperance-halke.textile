<!-- OP_BIO -->
*29th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*

Present at the funeral of [[:micycle-smynth | Micycle Smynth]]. One of the two archive staff [[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Ros]] spotted in dark robes during the service. Temperance and [[:seelie-ophelan | Seelie Ophelan]] collected [[:micycle-smynth | Micycle]]'s remains after the cold flame stripped the flesh and carried them toward the room where [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Deathwalker]] and [[:amanira | Amanira]] were exploring — narrowly avoided when the party fled.

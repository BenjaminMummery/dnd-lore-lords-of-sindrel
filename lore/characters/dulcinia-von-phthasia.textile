<!-- OP_DESCRIPTION -->
Dulcinea is a slim, pallid young woman with dark hair. Her illness would be evident in each unsteady step and hesitant breath even if she did not frequently cough blood into a lace handkerchief.

Her illness has prevented her from any form of travelling or outdoors activities for much of her life, and her sedentary existence has left her desperately bored. She lives vicariously through the tales of other people's exploits, and makes little distinction between the fictional characters in her books and real people.

<!-- OP_BIO -->
* *10th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*
** Accompanied by her bodyguard [[:pthul | Pthul]], Dulcinia departed the von Phthasia estate in [[Soulwind | Soulwind]] with the goal of travelling to [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] to seek a magical cure for her ailment. The going was slow due to Dulcinia's ill health and the large amount of gold and jewels they were transporting.
* *24th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*
** After a rag-tag group of adventurers recovered her purse from some ruffians, Dulcinea invited them to share her table and listened in rapt attention to their stories.[1]
* *25th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*
** Pthul and Dulcinia's coach is ambushed by [[Anathemists | Anathemists]].[1]
** Survived the coach going over the cliff; fainted during the crash; continued with the party through flooded [[Fallowhurst | Fallowhurst]] and [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]].[2][3]
* *26th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])*
** Traveled with the party from the [[Fallowhurst Outpost | Fallowhurst Outpost]] to [[Sindrel | Sindrel]].[3]
** Parted company with the Adventurers after their arrival in the city (afternoon); sought treatment at the [[Houses of Healing | Houses of Healing]].[4]

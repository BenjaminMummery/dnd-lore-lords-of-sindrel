Demeletas is a Tiefling, his purple skin almost the same shade as the robes he wears. Although his eyes are a milky white, he appears to have no issue navigating his surroundings.

He speaks in declaratives that sound a lot like fortune cookie platitudes. What wisdom they contain is typically only clear in hindsight.

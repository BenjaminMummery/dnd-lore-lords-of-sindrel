h2. A Convenient Lie

_Evening of the 29th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])_

[[:commander-locke | Prefect Locke]] wanted answers. The party offered an acceptable lie: they had wandered into the catacombs beneath an unspecified [[Osteomantic Archives | Osteomantic Archive]] more or less at random, become lost, and emerged where the tunnels led.

Locke seemed perturbed both at the apparently unknown breach in Oestran security, and that they had not followed his suggestion to investigate local weaponsmiths. The [[The Adventurers | Wonch]], apparently enamoured of a bit of karmic justice, took that as their cue to bully Locke into permitting them to conduct a stock-take of [[House Oestra | House Oestra]]'s armouries. Nothing appeared amiss.

Locke directed them to their new base of operations: [[Radwasp House | Radwasp House]], a [[House Lithra | Lithra]] townhouse in upper [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] set aside for [[Hexine Circle | Hexine Circle]] investigators - more rooms than strictly necessary, and a butler named [[:cedric | Cedric]] who received their arrival with the calm of a man who had seen worse. Locke had been tasked to pass on the keys; the lodging itself was the Circle's arrangement, not Oestra's hospitality.

h2. Downtime

_Morning through afternoon of the 30th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])_

After a much-needed rest, the party scattered across the city for a quieter day of errands and reflection.

h3. [[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Rossin "Ros" Greyhirst]]

Ros sought materials for a new weapon - some manner of chain-and-spearhead contraption she hoped [[:journeyman-galoras | Journeyman Galoras]] might assemble. A letter arrived from [[:navinea-senetera | Navinea Senetera]]:

bq). Dear Rossin Greyhirst,
<br>
I extend my thanks for your conversation at the [[Hexine Circle | Hexine Circle]]. I found it to be most informative, and it is my belief that further exploration of application of systems found in natural environments to [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]'s unique ecology would prove fruitful. To that end, I would like to extend an invitation for yourself and any of your compatriots who so choose to visit the Amalthean and converse with our leading factors.
<br>
Yours,
[[:navinea-senetera | Navinea Senetera]]

h3. [[:silrie-aegiskiir | Silrie Aegiskiir]]

Silrie sought [[Knuckledusters | magical knuckle-dusters]], then visited the [[Temple of the Dawn Father | Temple of the Dawn Father]]. In meditation they received an ominous vision warning of dangers on the road ahead.

h3. [[:amanira | Amanira]]

[[:amanira | Amanira]] hunted for books on the city's history, piecing together the official story of seven great mages and the traitor eighth from tomes written by Sindrelians for Sindrelians.

One book differed - _The Rise and Fall of Houd_ is a dense historical treatise on [[The Magedom of Houd | the Magedom of Houd]], the empire that once united the northern continent. Some of the contemporary documents it quotes make reference to a magical disturbance in the far north, and one refers to a party of investigators being dispatched from [[The Soulwind Institute | the Soulwind Institute]].

h3. [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Nakoma Deathwalker Mor'got'ha]] and [[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin Ravenglade]]

The pair went clothes shopping. It was, by the standards of their recent adventures, almost peaceful.

h3. [[:ayr | Ayr]]

Always hunting for better gear, [[:ayr | Ayr]] met [[:kelferria-the-smith | Kelferria the Smith]] to discuss commissioning plate armour. She took his measurements and said she would send a formal quote in due course - she was already overworked by some large order, which is a detail worth remembering. No money changed hands; the Wonch have not yet decided whether to accept whatever she proposes.

[[:journeyman-galoras | Journeyman Galoras]] called at [[Radwasp House | Radwasp House]] to deliver [[:ayr | Ayr]]'s new weapon: [[The Gentleman's Greeting | The Gentleman's Greeting]] — a force-multiplying blade that gathered a single charge each day and could, in principle, release considerably more power than any sane person ought to wield in a parlour.

h3. [[:pin | Pin]]

[[:pin | Pin]] received an invitation from [[:hielaman | Captain Hielaman]]:

bq). Pin,
<br>
Your ill informed and backwards-thinking opinions on [[Ditchquer | ditchquer]] must be remedied. I have a season box, and qualifiers begin this week. Kindly join me for a few matches so that I may educate you and your friends as to why the Griffon's Talons are the absolute peak of sporting performance and all other teams are trash.
<br>
Hielaman

h2. [[Radwasp House | Radwasp House]]

_Evening of the 30th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])_

Back at the townhouse, [[:cedric | Cedric]] had built a like-for-like miniature of the building for [[:razorbeak | the chicken]] to inhabit. The cat, which had seemed out of sorts earlier, was gone.

A letter for [[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin]] announced provisional acceptance at [[The Heptade Academy | The Heptade Academy]], contingent on completion of the party's current investigation. Spurred by the news, Wilrin spent the evening in [[Radwasp House | Radwasp House]]'s magical training room practising control of the wild energies that coursed through him.

[[:ayr | Ayr]] attempted to remove [[The Unanswered Cry | The Unanswered Cry]], taken from the [[Shrine of the Queen of Ravens | shrine of the Queen of Ravens]], and found he could not. That night he dreamed of wings and rivers of blood running through the streets of the upper city - the vital fluids of the rich, pumping beneath pavements the poor were trodden into.

[[:amanira | Amanira]], deep in meditation, was visited by a cat.

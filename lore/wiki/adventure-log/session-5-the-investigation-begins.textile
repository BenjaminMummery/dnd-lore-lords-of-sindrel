h2. The [[Hexine Circle | Hexine Circle]]

_Morning of the 29th of Enean, year 4218 ([[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]])_

[[The Adventurers | The Adventurers]] were summoned by the [[Hexine Circle | Hexine Circle]].

bq). The building to which you have been summoned is several levels up - the messenger has a carriage ready - a two-wheeled affair drawn by a horse made of shadows. Such conveyances are a common sight in the city, although this one is conspicuous in its finery.
<br>
The journey takes about 15 minutes. You leave the comparatively mundane merchants quarter behind, the buildings becoming noticeably grander as you wend your way upwards. Finally you arrive at a building on one of the upper levels. The structure is a hexagonal tower topped by four inward curving spires. At the apex hangs a large bell.
<br>

The circle turned out to be a lesser council comprised of mid-ranking representatives of each of the Houses. Specifically:
    * [[:seer-demeletas | Seer Demeletas]] ([[House Goela | House Goela]])
    * [[:hielaman | Captain Hielaman]] ([[House Meness | House Meness]])
    * [[:alandria | Caller Alandria]]  ([[House Beltus | House Beltus]])
    * [[:commander-locke | Prefect Locke]] ([[House Oestra | House Oestra]])
    * [[:emissary-halan | Emissary Halan]] ([[House Lithra | House Lithra]])
    * [[:journeyman-galoras | Journeyman Galoras]] ([[House Mabon | House Mabon]])
    * [[:navinea-senetera | Navinea Senetera]] ([[House Grimbolg | House Grimbolg]])

[[:emissary-halan | Emissary Halan]] began the meeting:

bq). Thank you for coming. Allow me to make the introductions. I am [[:emissary-halan | Emissary Halan]] of [[House Lithra | House Lithra]]. I believe you already know [[:hielaman | Captain Hielaman]] of [[House Meness | House Meness]]. The rest of our company are [[:commander-locke | Prefect Locke]] representing [[House Oestra | House Oestra]], [[:alandria | Caller Alandria]] of [[House Beltus | House Beltus]], [[:seer-demeletas | Seer Demeletas]] of [[House Goela | House Goela]], [[:navinea-senetera | Navinea Senetera]] representing [[House Grimbolg | House Grimbolg]], and finally representing [[House Mabon | House Mabon,]] [[:journeyman-galoras | Journeyman Galoras]]. We are the [[Hexine Circle | Hexine Circle]], a council of representatives of each House convened in order to address a specific threat to our city. Convened, in fact, as a direct result of your actions.
<br>
We understand that you are the brave travellers who not only survived [[Fallowhurst | Fallowhurst]] but brought us an Anathemist Lieutenant, this is no small feat and you have both our admiration and our gratitude. It was the results of [[:hielaman | Captain Hielaman]]'s preliminary interrogation that brought to light the necessity for this council. Captain?

 _Captain Hielaman_:
bq). Indeed. Amid a slew of invective and hollow defiance, not to mention a number of imaginative speculations as to my ancestry, our prisoner made reference to some highly privileged information. Information that could only be acquired from a high ranking member of one of the houses.

 _Halan_:
bq). Quite. You see our problem - we have long suspected that anathamist spies have infiltrated the city, but now it appears that they have gained access to highly sensitive individuals and information. We have a pressing need to route out this corruption. And that is where you come in.
<br>
We also understand that you have not, as of yet, secured the patronage of one of the houses, and that your time in our great city is therefore limited. You are also clearly capable, resilient, and no friends to the anathamists. As new arrivals you are comparatively unknown and therefore able to go places and gain confidences that our more official agents would find impossible. Our offer to you is this: seek out the spies, bring them to justice, and you may have your pick of patronages. What say you?

[[:emissary-halan | Halan]] added, when the party asked where they should begin:

bq). Ah. This is another motiviation for our decision to bring in outside help. The council has failed to reach a consensus on the best approach. I'd advise that you discuss the matter with each of my colleagues at your convenience.

The [[Hexine Circle | Hexine Circle]] requested the aid of [[The Adventurers | The Adventurers]] in rooting out Anathemist spies. The [[:arise-to-victory-over-the-infidel-hoards | lieutenant]] they captured made reference to highly sensitive information that could only be obtained by infiltrating one of the houses.

h2. Gathering leads

_Immediately afterward, the same morning_

The [[The Adventurers | adventurers]] split up to interview each Factor and gather potential leads.

h3. [[House Goela | House Goela]]
[[File:1554071  | class=media-item-align-right | 300px | demeletas.jpeg]]

bq). Your path leads through earth and ash and brimstone, but beware the fire and fury – its blinding light hides the spectre of death. It is easy to get lost in the dark, but remember to trust in righteousness. Your lucky number is eight. Your lucky colour is red. Pay close attention to your friends, you may find yourself looking to them at times you don’t expect. Avoid shellfish, but be open to trying new experiences.

[[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Deathwalker]] and [[:silrie-aegiskiir | Silrie]] started by talking to [[:seer-demeletas | Seer Demeletas]]. His guidance was cryptic rather than necessarily useful, apparently the result of him "reading the bones". [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Deathwalker]] took a flirtatious approach, securing a date with the tiefling.

When asked which other house would be most likely to be involved:

bq). Life's challenges are often unforeseen by those who keep the closest watch. But _quis custodiet ipsos custodes_? Quis indeed.

<hr>

h3. [[House Mabon | House Mabon]]

[[File:1554073  | class=media-item-align-left | 400px | galoras.png]]

bq). Incidents, the last months. Misfortune maybe, sabotage we fear. Appearing random, but always a design beneath. The random hides the pattern. Find the parts that do not fit, pull on them until deception unravels.

When asked which other house would be most likely to be involved:

bq). Willingly? None. But by deceit? Perhaps Beltus would be their target - concerned with position and authority. Ambitious, but precarious.

[[:ayr | Ayr]] found that [[:journeyman-galoras | Journeyman Galoras]] was eager to be helpful, if lacking in social confidence. They were willing to leverage the prodigious creative powers of [[House Mabon | House Mabon]] to the benefit of [[The Investigation | the investigation]]. Ayr requested a weapon, at which Galoras started talking cryptically about "force multipliers" — the commission that became [[The Gentleman's Greeting | The Gentleman's Greeting]]. They also gave [[:ayr | Ayr]] a gift - a small device they'd been fiddling with that constantly rotated under its own power.

The "incidents" [[:journeyman-galoras | Galoras]] mentioned were a series of suspicious happenings going back several months. These included:

* The control rod of one of [[House Grimbolg | House Grimbolg]]'s water generators was inverted, causing a water storage tank to overflow. This resulting in the drowning of one of their [[Factor | factors]], [[:dogara | Hydromancer D'gara]], as well as significant losses from the adjacent storage silos.
* [[:nielsenn | Nielsenn]], [[Adjutant | adjutant]] of [[House Mabon | House Mabon]], was vaporised when one of his own trapped locks misfired.
* A summoning being carried out by  [[:caller-georg |Caller Johann Georg]], [[Factor | factor]] of [[House Beltus | House Beltus]], went badly wrong. The demon was able to break free of the summoning circle and possess the summoner. Under its influence, [[:caller-georg | Caller Georg]] ran amok through the halls of the [[Beltan Institute | Beltan Institute]] defacing a number of portraits and other precious items before his heat finally gave out. As a final sick joke, the demon enchanted its own sulphurous leavings which, when gathered up with the rest of the day's refuse, exploded. The page of his newly purchased grimoire showing the appropriate summoning circle for a succubus was found to have been vandalised.
* The punch ladle at a diplomatic function being hosted by [[House Lithra | House Lithra]] was exchanged with one that had been enchanted with a geis of truth speaking. This resulted in three major diplomatic incidents, the end of two marriages, and a very awkward proposal. House [[Factor | factor]] [[:emissary-isius | Emissary Isius]] has had to withdraw from public life.
* A series of explosive devices were found in the sewers below [[The Fulcrum | The Fulcrum]] on the night of the [[Ditchquer | Ditchquer]] tournament closing ceremony. One detonated early, causing some minor structural damage. The others were discovered and diffused. High ranking [[Factor | Factors]] of each house were in attendance, any or all of whom may have been the intended target. Reinforcements from [[House Oestra | House Oestra]] were called in to support the [[House Meness | House Meness]] guards already in place.
* [[:f-lar-mnementh | Tyromancer Pterath]], [[Factor | factor]] of [[House Goela | House Goela]], was poisoned. Investigators determined that a large dosage of hallucinogens had been added to his cheese. Pterath survived, but has been unwilling to attempt any further acts of divination.
* [[:craftsman-shegrim | Craftsman Shegrim]], a high-level [[Factor | factor]] of [[House Mabon | House Mabon]], was  commissioned anonymously to place a spell of reflection on an amulet. The amulet had _already_ been enchanted with a spell of reflection, causing the enchantment to rebound. In a city where magic is everywhere, he's a walking danger to everyone around him.
* A [[House Meness | House Meness]] [[Factor | factor]], [[:major-vindas | Major Vindas]], was killed by piece of armour with an inverted reflection enchantment – instead of reversing incoming force it amplified it. When she invited an aide to help her test the armour with a low level firebolt spell, she was incinerated instantly.

<hr>

h3. [[House Meness | House Meness]]

[[File:1554074  | class=media-item-align-right | 300px | hieleman.jpg]]

bq). Shake down the local criminals. Our "spies" need ways to move about the city, ways to get their hands on information, equipment, resources, all without being noticed. That requires a lot of familiarity with the city. Why start from scratch when you can use whats already here? Somewhere out there are some mid- to high-level crooks who are suddenly much better paid as a reward for the treason. Below them are petty scum looking to get rich. They’re the weak link, find them and lean on them.

Having established a relationship with [[:hielaman | Captain Hielaman]], [[:pin | Pin]] elected to approach the [[House Meness | House Meness]] representative. The captain was surprisingly sanguine about their previous altercation, apparently viewing it as a form of blowing off steam. He mentioned that the face of organised crime in the city is a group known as the [[The Black Cats | Black Cats]], and that mid-level crooks there are worth shaking down. A direct assault on their leadership, he added, had been counselled against upstairs; better to lean on the petty scum at the bottom and see what breaks.

<hr>

h3. [[House Lithra | House Lithra]]

[[File:1554075  | class=media-item-align-left | 300px | Halan.jpg]]

bq). The only true power is information, and the key to information is access. They can only have learned what they know from highly placed individuals - 20 or so in the entire city I suspect. If you want to get access to those sorts of social circles, you need to seem like you belong. I'd imaging you could get into one of my soirees with a wink and a suitably daring fake name, but the upper circles are a different story. You would need money, and lots of it. Enough for an opulent lifestyle  and even more for bribes and other social lubrication. So too you would need to appear to be socially influential and reliably stable - old money gentry, or hereditary nobility, but with a legend that is unlikely to contradict anyone's experiences. They couldn't risk changing alias within the city, they'll have arrived in character. I would suggest looking at recent arrivals, within the last few months. Focus on the nobility and wealthier merchants - people who can move large amounts of money without arousing suspicion. Finally look for people who are obviously harmless, someone around whom it would be easy to drop your guard. For example, a young naive noblewoman with a disarmingly eager affect travelling with a fortune in gold and an almost comically proficient bodyguard...
<br>
I jest of course, I have confirmed [[:dulcinia-von-phthasia | the Lady Von Pthasia']]s identity myself, and either way she only arrived in the city _after_ the information loss had occurred. Although, I must confess may be inclined to use certain details of her life the next time I myself have to go undercover.

Having concluded their conversation with [[:seer-demeletas | Seer Demeletas]], [[:silrie-aegiskiir | Silrie]] and [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | DW]] next approached [[:emissary-halan | Emissary Halan]], joined by [[:ayr | Ayr]]. In addition to her advice, she agreed to provide [[:silrie-aegiskiir | Silrie]] with blueprints of the oldest structures in the city (although she clarified that there isn't a central authority for these things, each house holding its own records).

<hr>

h3. [[House Beltus | House Beltus]]

[[File:1554077  | class=media-item-align-right | 300px | alandria.jpg]]

bq). The [[Osteomantic Archives | Osteomantic Archives]]. Think about it – they must have a base of operations – somewhere their comings and goings won’t be noticed. Somewhere well trafficked but anonymous. People go in and out of the archives all the time, but nobody stays long. And, nobody thinks much of it if you’re a bit strange. The catacombs are a labyrinth, and they’re chronically understaffed. You could hide anything down there.”

[[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin]] and [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | DW]]'s conversation with [[:alandria | Caller Alandria]] did not get off to a good start. The [[House Beltus | House Beltus]] representative was clearly frustrated with the whole process, and this irritation led to a contentious discourse.

At several points, she dispatched her Mephit companion to retrieve information for her. One of these was that the least trafficked [[Osteomantic Archives | Osteomantic Archive]] in the city is the one in [[The Wallarch District | The Wallarch District]].

<hr>

h3. [[House Oestra | House Oestra]]

[[File:1554079  | class=media-item-align-left | 300px | locke.jpg]]

bq). If they’re here, they must be planning to use their position to strike against the city. That means weapons, either smuggled in or acquired here. We should be checking out every armoury and weapon shop in the city, see if any shipments have come up short or inventory has gone missing.

[[:commander-locke | Prefect Locke]] proved to be a gruff, no nonsense warrior, the only ostentation he wore being a series of three earrings in his left ear. In addition to his advice, he also had a request - [[:lacerta-kirrahe | Captain Kirrahe]], the leader of the mercenaries who was slain by [[Anathemists | Anathemists]] back in session 1 had been a personal friend of his, and he was very interested in any information that could lead him to the killer.

<hr>

h3. [[House Grimbolg | House Grimbolg]]

[[File:1554081  | class=media-item-align-right | 300px | Senetere.jpg]]

bq). There is no point closing the stable door when the horse has already bolted. We should be focussing on increasing security around critical infrastructure in order to prevent the next attack.

[[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Ros]] spent much of this time in silent companionship with the [[House Grimbolg | House Grimbolg]] representative, [[:navinea-senetera | Navinea Senetera]] . The transmuter proved to be a contemplative soul. When asked about whether the region around [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] was naturally barren or had been in some way affected by the city, she gave this considerable thought before concluding that she had no way of knowing.

There followed an in-depth discussion around the ecology of the city. [[:navinea-senetera | Senetera]] was deeply interested in [[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Ros's]] knowledge of natural systems, and was actively looking for insights that might improve the efficiency of [[House Grimbolg | House Grimbolg]]'s operation. They've been stretched pretty thin recently with increased demand.

h2. Next Steps

Given that [[The Black Cats | The Black Cats]] and the most likely [[Osteomantic Archives | Osteomantic Archive]] were both located in [[The Wallarch District | The Wallarch District]], [[The Adventurers | the Wonch]] elected to begin their investigation there.

Horology system originally devised by the early inhabitants of [[Soulwind | Soulwind]] before being widely adopted across [[Arcan | Arcan]].

Like Earth, Arcan has a 365-day year. The Soulwind calendar divides this into 10 months of 36 days, plus a 5-day festival at midwinter. The year begins on the winter solstice.

The calendar counts years since the (supposed) date of the city's founding. The current year is 4218.

Fruit of [[Zotikó | Zotikó]], the world of Vitality.

During earnest post-stabilisation exploration (~**3230s SC**), the founders discovered the peaches on [[Zotikó | Zotikó]] — guarded, and not accessible without bloodshed. They were not taken then.

After the calamity at [[Fallowhurst | Fallowhurst]] (~**3238 SC**), an early **Riftlock** prototype (~**3242 SC**) let the Circle steal the fruit by force, leaving devastation in [[Zotikó | Zotikó]]. **Six** founders consumed peaches within the founding generation. [[:first-of-mabon | First of Mabon]] achieved immortality through peach-based artifice ([[The Amulet of Stars | The Amulet of Stars]]) instead of eating.

[[:mistress-lucretia-samhain | Mistress Lucretia Samhain]] refused and revealed her vampirism — [[Thanismot | Thanismot]] already nullified her thirst and sunlight vulnerability; she would not bind herself to Vitality's gift.

No peaches are known to remain.

**Current holder:** None (consumed ~3242 SC).

Officially known as Granary Ward and colloquially referred to simply as "the Cornucopia," this district serves as the logistical heart of [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]. Food, fuel, construction materials, and countless other necessities pass through its warehouses and depots before being distributed throughout the city. Though less glamorous than [[Dockward | Dockward]] or [[Merrylane | Merrylane]], few districts are more important to Sindrel's continued survival. The district's rhythms are dictated by deliveries, inventories, and harvests, and its people possess a quiet confidence born from knowing that the rest of the city depends upon them.

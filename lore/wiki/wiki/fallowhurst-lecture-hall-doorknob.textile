A heavy brass doorknob, still threaded on its spindle — prised from the side door of the [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]] lecture hall (room C) when [[:ayr | Ayr]] forced the door open on the 25th of Enean 4218 SC.[1]

The college was already a ruin; the theft left splintered wood and a gap where the latch had been. [[:ayr | Ayr]] pocketed it as a souvenir. No enchantment is known — yet.

**Current holder:** [[:ayr | Ayr]].

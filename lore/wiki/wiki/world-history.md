For the vast majority of world history, magic has been the dominant form of power. The world has been shaped around it, with magical strength becoming the primary shaping force of politics. Over the last few centuries, however, it has become undeniable that magic is dying out. As the previously iron-clad power structures sought to shore up their suddenly crumbling positions, wars and revolutions become increasingly common. 

The northern regions of the continent of Artresh are a patchwork of city states and petty kingdoms, the fractured remains of the once semi-unified [[The Magedom of Houd | Magedom of Houd]]. Following the Seven-Day War with the [[The Thaumocracy of Tellerin | Tellerites]], the Mage Lord Council governing the Magedom, never the most cohesive group at the best of times, fractured. The resulting states are embroiled in an ever-shifting network of alliance, feud, political marriage, and, occasionally, actual war. [[Soulwind | Soulwind]], on the northern coast, is the largest of these states by area, although its territory consists mostly of inhospitable mountainous land with little in the way of resources. 

To the south is the mysterious [[The Thaumocracy of Tellerin | Thaumocracy of Tellerin]], a once-great and expansionist empire. After their unsuccessful attempt to invade Houd, the empire retreated, closing its borders to outsiders.

h2. Timeline of World History

h3. *3200s SC* (roughly 1000 years ago)

* _Researchers from the Soulwind Institute discover a dimensional tear in the far north of the continent. The Soulwind Institute Dimensional Research and Exploration Laboratory (SInDREL) is established to study it._ 
* _The researchers find their way to Vitality and consume the Peaches of Immortality._
* _Forseeing the profitability of Sindrel and growing tired of travelling between Soulwind and Sindrel, [[:lord-ablas-oestra | Lord Ablas Oestra]] establishes the Oestra estate at [[Fallowhurst | Fallowhurst]]._
* _The researchers harvest resources and riches from the other worlds, establishing Sindrel as an economic hub._

*3500s SC* (roughly 700 years ago)
* _Growing frustrated by the limitations of the dimensional tear, the Lords of Sindrel persuade the Soulwind Institute to establish the magical conduit between their cities. What they originally pitched as a two-way connection allowing the two sites to easily share power as needed was in fact designed to quietly harvest power for Sindrel._

*3700s SC* (roughly 500 years ago)
* _Having harvested sufficient power from the conduit, the Lords of Sindrel pour massive amounts of energy into the tear in order to stabilise it._
* _Now able to control which worlds they travel too, they bias towards Kosmios for extractive purposes. The compounding magical intrusion causes the Wheel of Forms to destabilise._ 
* _[[:lord-ablas-oestra | Lord Oestra]] moves the seat of his family to Sindrel, turning the [[Fallowhurst | Fallowhurst]] estate over to his son [[:embren-oestra | Embren]]_

*3702 SC* (491 years ago)
* _[[:embren-oestra | Embren Oestra]] establishes the Fallowhurst College to study healing magic._

*3715 SC* (503 years ago)
* _The calamity at [[Fallowhurst | Fallowhurst]] - pushed by [[:lord-ablas-oestra | Lord Oestra]] to experiment with dangerous magics, the scholars of the [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]] are killed in a magical disaster._

*3760s SC* (roughly 450 years ago)
* _The last survivors of [[Fallowhurst | Fallowhurst]] leave._

*3850s SC* (roughly 350 years ago)
* The first stirrings of the death of magic are felt.

*circa 3900 SC* (roughly 300 years ago)
* _With magic becoming rarer in [[Arcan | Arcan]] and beginning to appear naturally in Kosmios, Sindrel seeks to lock the rift permanently to Kosmios and begin harvesting magical resources as well as the more mundane offerings to bolster their diminishing powers._ 
* Soulwind's population begins to diminish as magic users drift towards Sindrel.
* [[The Fall of the Eighth House | The Fall of the Eighth House]] - [[:mistress-lucretia-samhain | Mistress Lucretia Samhain]] becomes disaffected with Sindrel's colonial ambitions and prepares to move against the circle. They imprison her in Thanismot.

*3953 SC* (roughly 250 years ago) - The Seven Day War
* Fought between the The Mage lords of Houd and the Thaumocracy of Tellerin
* _With magical power growing scarce, especially in the south, the Tellerites launched an invasion of Houd._
* The decisive battle was fought on the Planes of Herrman, where a stalemate between the attacking Tellerites and the beleagured Houdians was ended by the timely arrival of Houdian reinforcements. The bulk of the Tellerite forces were delayed by the Battle of Brassmeade Bridge where, according to the tales, a single bezerker held the narrow bridge against the vastly superior force for a full day.
* [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Nakoma Deathwalker Mor'got'ha]] character is slain and resurrected.

*~4150 SC* (roughly 50 years ago)
* [[Project Kosmios | Project Kosmios]] is instantiated - the Lords of Sindrel begin to plan to move the entire city into the now magical world of Kosmios.

A magic circle forming part of a binding spell. An incomplete version was found on the blackboard in the Lecture Hall of [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]].

[[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin Ravenglade]] attempted to use the sigil by employing [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Nakoma Deathwalker Mor'got'ha]]'s blood. The discharge branded both with matching sigil scars — on Wilrin's face and Deathwalker's back.

<!-- GM_INFO_START -->
h3. The Binding

Wilrin's blood-magic attempt linked him and Deathwalker to the centuries-old binding spell. Both bear permanent sigil scars. The link has one known mechanical consequence:

Whenever Deathwalker takes damage, Wilrin rolls a d8. On a 1, Deathwalker takes half damage and Wilrin takes the rest.

Further effects are unknown — the spell outlasted the death of magic itself.

Personal key to [[:silas-oestra | Silas Oestra]]'s chambers within the [[House Oestra | Oestra Leageur]], usable via the [[Osteomantic Archives | archive]] route as well as conventional doors.

During the [[Ditchquer | Ditchquer]] closing ceremony, [[:barnabus | Barnabus]] pickpocketed this key and passed it to [[:arouraios | Arouraios]] (disguised as a coachman). [[:georges-de-selve | Georges de Selve]] and [[:jean-de-dinteville | Jean de Dinteville]] collected it, entered with [[:nielsenn | Nielsenn]]'s locksmith skills and false [[House Oestra | Oestra]] armour, copied classified files from Silas's rooms, and stole what they could not copy. The breach is tied to [[Anathemists | Anathemist]] knowledge of [[Kosmios | Kosmios]].

Silas noticed the loss later and had the lock reissued; he did not search thoroughly until after further intelligence pointed to a high-level breach. He now knows the theft was through **his** custody but will not volunteer that fact.

**Current holder:** Unknown — likely with the departed conspirators ([[:georges-de-selve | Georges de Selve]] / [[:jean-de-dinteville | Jean de Dinteville]]). Silas carries a **replacement** key.

h2. The Age of Magic is Ending

Over the last century, the gradual decay of the supernatural has become undeniable. Mages who once reshaped reality now struggle with simple illusions; enchanted items that should last generations are exhausted after a handful of uses; even those who draw their power from [[The Gods | the gods]] have found their deities to be growing distant and uninterested.

As the old power structures crumble, new and ambitious ideologies compete for dominance. The chaos is growing, and the new shape of the world is up for grabs.

Born into this world, you have lived the majority of your lives with the knowledge that once concrete certainties are eroding around you. And you have all, for your own reasons, made the long, arduous journey to the one place on earth where wonders persist - [[Sindrel | Sindrel]], the last city of magic.

h3. The story so far

_Through Session 12 (31st Enean 4218 SC)._

Seven strangers met at [[The White Dragon Coaching Inn | the White Dragon]], bound for [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]. They survived an [[Anathemists | Anathemist]] ambush on the [[The Mage Road | Mage Road]], rode the [[Sindrel Coach | coach]] over a cliff rather than surrender, and stumbled into the drowned ruins of [[Fallowhurst | Fallowhurst]], where [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]] tried very hard to keep them.

On the plains before the city, they faced the Anathemists again. [[House Meness | House Meness]] took custody of the defeated leftenant, [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] took the travellers' names, and the [[Hexine Circle | Hexine Circle]] gave them a better offer than a short stay followed by exile: root out spies who had burrowed far deeper into the city than anyone admitted.

Calling themselves [[The Adventurers | the Wonch]], they followed the sabotage trail through [[The Wallarch District | Wallarch]], the [[Osteomantic Archives | Osteomantic Archives]], [[House Oestra | Oestra]] vaults, [[The Fulcrum | Fulcrum]] service tunnels, and the [[House Grimbolg | Cornucopia]]. Someone nearly brought down the Fulcrum not with sorcery, but with clockwork, flint, and gunpowder brewed from the city's own waste; the same hand may have drowned [[:dogara | Hydromancer D'gara]] to steal nitre and burned sulphur under cover of [[House Beltus | Beltus]]'s conjured "demons."

By the evening of the 31st, the Wonch had kept an Oestran summons waiting while they chased [[:chuggaz | Chuggaz]] into Wallarch and the back room of [[The Cutthroat's Repose | the Cutthroat's Repose]]. There, [[:sable-silvestre | Sable]] offered answers at a price, and the party paid with a dangerous demonstration of the deathly sigil that binds [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Deathwalker]] and [[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin]].

The Black Cats have promised information. [[House Oestra | House Oestra]] has been kept waiting. In [[Sindrel | Sindrel]], both are the kind of debt that accrues interest.

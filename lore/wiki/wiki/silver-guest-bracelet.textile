A silver band issued to visitors entering [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] without Great House patronage. When raised, it displays a map of the city and the number of days remaining on the wearer's authorised stay.

[[The Adventurers | The Wonch]] received silver bracelets on arrival (26 Enean 4218 SC) with **36 days** to find a patron or leave.

<!-- GM_INFO_START -->
Binding at the inner gate — spoken name, putting on the band, brief contact with an attuned gold bracelet or [[Factor | Factor]] tattoo — completes a ritual visitors rarely recognise as magic.

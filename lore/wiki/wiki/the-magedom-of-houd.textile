An empire that once spanned much of the northern reaches of the continent including [[Soulwind | Soulwind]].

Houd was ruled by The Mage Lords, a coalition of magically and economically powerful families dating back to the ancient feudal period.

In [[World History | 3953]] [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]] (roughly 250 years ago), the Magedom was invaded by [[The Thaumocracy of Tellerin | The Thaumocracy of Tellerin]]. While the Houdis were ultimately victorious, the war was economically and politically draining. The Magedom, already sapped by the slow death of magic, eventually disintegrated into independent city states as individual mage lords attempted to hold onto their positions of privilege.

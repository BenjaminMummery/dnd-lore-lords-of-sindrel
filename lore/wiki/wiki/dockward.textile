Dockward surrounds the Annulus, the vast ring-shaped structure that serves as both [[House Mabon | House Mabon]]'s headquarters and [[Sindrel | Sindrel's]] principal airship port. Cargo cranes, customs offices, workshops, and merchant exchanges cluster around the great docking facilities, creating one of the busiest districts in the city. Goods from across the world arrive and depart through Dockward each day, making it one of the most cosmopolitan places in [[Artresh | Artresh]]. To stand upon the Annulus and watch the endless procession of airships is to understand why Sindrel remains the centre of a fading magical age.

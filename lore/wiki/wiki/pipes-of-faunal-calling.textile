A set of pan flutes sold at a [[House Mabon | House Mabon]] journeyman fair near the [[Temple of the Dawn Father | Temple of the Dawn Father]]. They carry a minor enchantment that allows a beast who has heard their song once to hear it from any distance and instantly know the location of the player.

[[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Ros]] purchased them on the 28th of Enean 4218 SC while [[:silrie-aegiskiir | Silrie]] prayed at the temple.

**Current holder:** [[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Rossin "Ros" Greyhirst]].

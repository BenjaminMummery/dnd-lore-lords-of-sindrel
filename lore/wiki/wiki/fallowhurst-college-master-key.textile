A heavy iron master key to the interior doors of [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]]. It opens most administrative and residential locks in the ruined college; some suites (including [[:embren-oestra | Embren Oestra]]'s) may require their own keys.

The party did not recover it on their first visit. It remains somewhere in the college for a return expedition.

**Current holder:** At [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]] (not yet found by [[The Adventurers | the Wonch]]).

A skin bag containing a set of polyhedral dice carved from bone, bought from a bin of random curios in a [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] magical shop on the 28th of Enean 4218 SC. The shopkeep noted that [[The Heptade Academy | The Heptade Academy]] uses similar foci for students who need help channelling unstable magic — though academy entry is restricted to [[Adjutant | Adjutants]] and [[Factor | Factors]].

**Current holder:** [[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin Ravenglade]].

[[File:1544263  | class=media-item-align-right | 240px-Poison_Mushroom.jpg]]

Sporeshrooms are a poisonous fungus native to the northern regions of Artresh. If disturbed, they release a cloud of toxic spores. Those that fall prey to the poison provide a rich bed in which the spores can grow. As a result, it is not uncommon to find sporeshrooms growing in a gradually expanding field of corpses.

h3. Encounters

Sporeshrooms were found growing in the orchard at [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]].

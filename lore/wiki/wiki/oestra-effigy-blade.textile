The silver greatsword set in the hands of the [[House Oestra | House Oestra]] founder effigy in the Hall of Founders, [[Osteomantic Archives | Osteomantic Archives]] — part of the statuary. Cold ornamental weight: no edge worth speaking of, no balance a fighter would trust, no evocation.

[[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Deathwalker]] pried it from the figure's grip and wrapped cloth around the distinctive cross-guard and hilt to hide the house geometry.[1]

**Current holder:** [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Nakoma Deathwalker Mor'got'ha]].

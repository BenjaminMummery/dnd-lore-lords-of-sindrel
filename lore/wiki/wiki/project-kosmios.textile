[[Kosmios | Kosmios]] is the focus of Sindrel’s current secret colonial project.

The modern plan is to physically move Sindrel into Kosmios in response to the looming death of magic.

Sindrel’s power is sustained by extractive magical colonialism in Kosmios.

The relocation spell requires immense magic and necromantic balance.

This necromantic component is missing because of the fall of House Samhain.

Samhain is unaware of the modern relocation plan.

The Hexine Circle does not know the truth.

Very high clearance only: founders, House heads / extremely senior insiders as appropriate.

Also include: why Anathemists knowing the word “Kosmios” is terrifying.

Artresh is the Houdian name for the continent on which our story takes place.

[[Sindrel | Sindrel]] is located at the extreme north-east of the continent.

Until about 250 years ago, the continent was split between two major powers - the [[The Magedom of Houd | The Magedom of Houd]] in the north, and [[The Thaumocracy of Tellerin | The Thaumocracy of Tellerin]] in the south. After the [[World History | Seven Day War]], the Magedom gradually split into a network of city states, while the Thaumocracy closed its borders and became, for all intents and purposes, terra incognita.

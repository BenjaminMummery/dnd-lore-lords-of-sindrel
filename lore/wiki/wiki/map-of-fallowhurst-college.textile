Floor plan of [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]], bearing the legend "Fallowhurst College of Healing." A large framed map once hung in the inner lobby; only the ground floor remained legible even before the college fell.

In the Museum, [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Deathwalker]] recovered a partial section. It was enough to show that the grime trail through the entrance hall led to the Operating Theatre.[1]

**Current holder:** [[:nakoma-deathwalker-mor-got-ha | Nakoma Deathwalker Mor'got'ha]] (partial map only).

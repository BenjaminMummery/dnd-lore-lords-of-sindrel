Research notes on [[Triffid | triffid]] cultivation at [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]]. Scholars bred aggressive greenhouse strains for medicinal oil and attempted hardier crops for the [[Arkay Mountain Range | Arkay]] soils; the latter produced hunting plants instead of food.

Found in the Specimen Room (L) alongside preserved samples. The Laboratory (K) holds the mature triffid grown from an experimental seed.[1]

**Current holder:** At [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]], Specimen Room (L) (not yet recovered by [[The Adventurers | the Wonch]]).

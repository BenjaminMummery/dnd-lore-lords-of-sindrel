The midsummer enclave is a tiny pocket dimension home to the court of [[:king-titan | King Titan]] and [[:queen-aubryn | Queen Aubryn]].

<!-- GM_INFO_START -->
As magic died out in [[Arcan | Arcan]], [[The Fey | The Fey]] found it harder and harder to exist comfortably there. Many left for other planes, but the court of [[:king-titan | King Titan]] and [[:queen-aubryn | Queen Aubryn]] chose another path - they created an enclosed bubble of magic - a tiny pocket dimension endlessly looping a perfect day of midsummer - a place where magic would never die out.

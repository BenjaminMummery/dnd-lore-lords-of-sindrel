The triffid ranges from between 7 and 10 feet in height, has between 3 and 6 sticks surrounding its base and when unsecured is capable of self-locomotion.

Triffid precursors, typically 4 to 8 inches in height, can be found across [[Arcan | Arcan]]. Larger, more agressive varieties were cultivated in the greenhouses of [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]].

<!-- GM_INFO_START -->
Aggressive triffids encountered at [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]] were cultivated for the healing properties of their oil. Further breeding aimed at a crop hardy enough for the icy soils of the [[Arkay Mountain Range | Arkay mountain range]] instead produced a highly aggressive strain capable of hunting and killing people.

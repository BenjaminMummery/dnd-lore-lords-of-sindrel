An [[Anathemists | Anathemist]] arquebus used on the [[The Mage Road | Mage Road]] ambush. It is mundane — clockwork, flint, and gunpowder, not sorcery — and shares its explosive family with the devices found beneath [[The Fulcrum | the Fulcrum]].

**Current holder:** [[:commander-wake | the Anathemist Commander]] (at large).

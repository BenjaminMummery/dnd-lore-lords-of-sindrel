The skull from an articulated teaching skeleton in the Preparation Room of [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]]. The display paired an elven skeleton with a dragonborn one among rusted gurneys and preserved specimens.

[[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin Ravenglade]] purloined it during the party's first visit on the 25th of Enean 4218 SC.

**Current holder:** [[:wilrin-racenglade | Wilrin Ravenglade]].

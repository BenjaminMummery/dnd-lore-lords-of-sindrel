A pair of magical knuckledusters acquired by [[:silrie-aegiskiir | Silrie Aegiskiir]] during downtime in [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] on the 30th of Enean 4218 SC — part of the same shopping run that saw new clothes ordered and [[The Gentleman's Greeting | The Gentleman's Greeting]] delivered to [[Radwasp House | Radwasp House]].

Silrie had been seeking a focus suited to unarmed fighting rather than a conventional blade.

With a small gesture, the rings separate, allowing the hands to be used freely. Clenching the fist locks them back into place.

**Current holder:** [[:silrie-aegiskiir | Silrie Aegiskiir]].

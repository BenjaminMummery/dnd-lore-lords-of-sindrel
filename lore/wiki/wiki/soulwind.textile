Crumbling metropolis, once a seat of magical learning.

Soulwind is notable for [[The Soulwind Calendar | The Soulwind Calendar]], a horological system widely used across [[Arcan | Arcan]]. It is linked to its closest major neighbour, [[Sindrel | Sindrel]], by [[The Mage Road | The Mage Road]].

The estate of the von Phthasia family is in Soulwind, and it is from here that [[:dulcinia-von-phthasia | Dulcinia von Phthasia]] and her bodyguard [[:pthul | Pthul]] departed for [[Sindrel | Sindrel]].

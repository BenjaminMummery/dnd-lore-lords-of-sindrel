The World of Mortality

<!-- GM_INFO_START -->
Thanismot is the world of mortality, endings, death, memory, and final consequence. It is the metaphysical opposite of [[Zotikó | Zotikó]], the world of vitality.
If [[Zotikó | Zotikó]] contains the force that produced the peaches of immortality, then Thanismot contains the equal and opposite principle: the power by which even the undying may end.

h2. Samhain in Exile
Thanismot was meant to be [[:mistress-lucretia-samhain | Samhain's]] prison. Instead, it became her refuge.

Necromancy is extraordinarily powerful in Thanismot, because everything there counts as dead or death-aligned in some metaphysical sense. The Samhainite exiles have not remained a single uniform community. Some have built grand necropoleis. Some have settled into peaceful academia, studying an entire world of mortality. Others have reshaped themselves, or been reshaped by Thanismot's laws.

To the Seven, [[The Eighth House | House Samhain]] is a dead House. In Thanismot, it has become something stranger, older, and far less easily erased.

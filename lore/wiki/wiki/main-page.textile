h2. The Basics

There exists a multiverse of worlds, each governed by a singular Form or Idea. Our point of origin is 'Arcanity', the world governed by Magic.

[[Sindrel | Sindrel]] began as SinDReL — a semi-permanent research camp around the dimensional tear (**3218 SC**). After rift stabilisation (~**3228 SC**) and the **Soulwind conduit** (~**3226 SC**), earnest exploration opened [[Zotikó | Zotikó]], [[Thanismot | Thanismot]], and [[Kosmios | Kosmios]]. Kosmios wealth made the city independent; the Great Houses began as training institutions for arriving mages.

[[:lord-ablas-oestra | Lord Oestra]]'s pursuit of immortality drove [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]] toward disaster (~**3238 SC**). A **Riftlock** prototype (~**3242 SC**) allowed the theft of the [[Peaches of Immortality | peaches]] from [[Zotikó | Zotikó]]; six founders became immortal by eating the peaches, [[:first-of-mabon | First of Mabon]] by artifice. [[:mistress-lucretia-samhain | Samhain]] refused. **Riftlock** (~**3245 SC**) locked the rift to [[Kosmios | Kosmios]], trapped Samhain in [[Thanismot | Thanismot]], and destroyed the conduit.

Colonial extraction destabilised the Wheel of Forms. Magic is dying on [[Arcan | Arcan]] and arising in [[Kosmios | Kosmios]]; the founders now pursue [[Project Kosmios | Project Kosmios]] — moving the entire city into Kosmios.

h2. How do Gods work w.r.t. the wheel of worlds?

The nature of a god is that they exist across all possible worlds simultaniously. The form by which they express themselves is mediated by the dominant world form.
* In Arcanity, faith operates like magic, reflecting D&D's mechanical approach to holy powers.
* In Mundanity it is expressed in everyday actions - a subtle guidance that shapes events
* In Divinity they walk upon the earth alongside mortals, tangible and accessible

h2. The Real History of [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]

* SinDReG dispatched (**3218 SC**); SinDReL camp founded around the rift.
* **~3226 SC:** Soulwind conduit (two-way link).
* **~3228 SC:** Rift stabilisation — short windows, controlled exploration.
* **~3230s SC:** Kosmios trade; Great Houses as training institutions.
* **~3234 SC:** Oestra seat moves to Sindrel; [[Fallowhurst College | Fallowhurst College]] established.
* **~3238 SC:** Fallowhurst calamity — Oestra (mortal) pushes dangerous magic.
* **~3242 SC:** Peach theft (Riftlock prototype); six founders immortal; First by artifice; Samhain refuses.
* **~3245 SC:** Riftlock; [[The Fall of the Eighth House | Samhain's fall]]; conduit destroyed.
* **~3288 SC:** Fallowhurst village abandoned (~50 yr after calamity).
* **~3298 SC:** Levees fail; the Drowned Crossing.
* **3958 SC:** Seven Day War (~260 years ago).
* **~4185 SC:** Anathemists emerge.
* **~4217 SC:** Mad King of Corvus (Pin / Deathwalker).
* **~4213 SC:** Anathemist escalation.
* **~4150 SC:** [[Project Kosmios | Project Kosmios]].

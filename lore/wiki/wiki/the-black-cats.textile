Organised crime in [[Sindrel | Sindrel]], concentrated in [[The Wallarch District | the Wallarch district]]. The name is branding — the organisation is human (and the usual assortment of corrupt adjutants), not felines.

[[:hielaman | Captain Hielaman]] of [[House Meness | House Meness]] treats them as the city's principal criminal syndicate and advised [[The Adventurers | the Wonch]] to lean on low-level operatives rather than assault leadership directly.

h3. Known leads

* [[:emerich-surefoot | Emerich Surefoot]] — low-level enforcer.
* [[The Cutthroat's Repose | The Cutthroat's Repose]] — back room; password "Swordfish"; Emerich's superior operates from here.
* [[:chuggaz | Chugan "Chuggaz" Velm]] — [[The Fulcrum | Fulcrum]] servant; rumoured links (see [[The Investigation | The Investigation]]).

<!-- GM_INFO_START -->
h3. Relation to City Cats (felines)

Unrelated. **City Cats** are actual felines — the informal network [[:amanira | Amanira]] can speak to via wild shape or [[:rossin-ros-greyhirst | Ros]]'s _Speak with Animals_ (e.g. [[:pads-silently | Pads-Silently]], [[:hunts-by-night | Hunts-By-Night]]). **Black Cats** have never considered using them; from a cat's perspective Black Cats are just more people. Do not conflate the factions or tags.

h3. Internal ranks

| Rank | Role |
|---|---|
| **Strands** | Street muscle, gambling, lookout — [[:emerich-surefoot | Emerich Surefoot]] tier |
| **Counters** | Fixers — bracelet fraud, Stay Ledger tampering, smuggling |
| **Gild** | Operatives with real or forged gold; house-adjacent access — [[:chuggaz | Chuggaz]] |
| **Vault** | Leadership; kompromat; Cutthroat relay |

**[[:chuggaz | Chuggaz]]** — true **Gild**, not a red herring. Fences Fulcrum shift rosters, servant-route gossip, and ceremony-night staffing to Counters/Vault. Cover when revealed: [[House Grimbolg | Grimbolg]] [[Adjutant | Adjutant]].

**Cutthroat's Repose contact** — Vault-adjacent **Counter**; holds ledger extracts and adjutant kompromat; never commits on premises.

h3. [[:hielaman | Captain Hielaman]]

Personally straight-lace; unaware of Vault kompromat. His **superiors** in Meness are compromised — he feels institutional pressure not to move directly against Vault leadership, but interprets it as politics, not blackmail. That makes him dangerous to the Cats: an honest investigator with little to leverage if he starts poking past Strands and Counters. Vault should prefer to divert, discredit, or isolate him rather than try to own him.

Anathemist pipeline (Hielaman's thesis): pay Counters/Gild for stay fraud, servant access, and mundane logistics — not ideology.

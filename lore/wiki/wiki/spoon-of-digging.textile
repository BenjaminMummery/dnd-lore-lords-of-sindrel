A ordinary-looking tea spoon, enchanted so that it can excavate solid rock as easily as lift sugar (albeit in teaspoon-sized helpings).

[[:ayr | Ayr]] stole it from a noble soiree in [[Sindrel | Sindrel]] while disguised as "Fenton Woofrunner" on the 28th of Enean 4218 SC. Later tinkering unlocked its digging properties.

**Current holder:** [[:ayr | Ayr]].

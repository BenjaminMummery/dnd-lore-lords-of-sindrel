The Canted Terrace is an upper-level district associated with [[House Beltus | House Beltus]]. It is respectable but unfashionable, deliberately angled and screened away from [[Merrylane | Merrylane]] and the richest neighbourhoods. Its best-known institution is the [[Beltan Institute | Beltan Institute]], House Beltus's official centre for summoning, binding, banishment, and pact-work.

The Terrace is not secret, but it is discreet. It is the sort of place everyone important knows about and very few wealthy residents wish to see from their windows.

A network of service platforms are positioned below the Terrace. The magical refuse of a thousand summonings run down sealed waste pipes to be collected by recycling carts below. One of these still bears the scars of the sulphurous explosion that followed the death of [[:caller-georg | Caller Georg]].

The Vermillion Company are a group of professional soldiers for hire, typically employed as additional security for merchants.

In Enean of 4218 [[The Soulwind Calendar | SC]], the company were hired by [[The White Dragon Coaching Inn | The White Dragon Coaching Inn]] to protect the coaches to [[Sindrel | Sindrel]]. On the 25th of that month, they and the coach they were escorting were ambushed by [[Anathemists | Anathemists]] and their [[:lacerta-kirrahe | captain]] was slain.
